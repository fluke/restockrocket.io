Back in Stock SMS alerts achieve a 3x better conversion rate than traditional email alerts. Restock Rocket's PLUS plan allows you to send UNLIMITED email alerts for FREE and charges you $0.05 per back in stock SMS alerts. The PLUS plan also entitles you to priority support and access to all new features that will be released.

Starting from the date you subscribe to the Plus plan, you get to send UNLIMITED back in stock emails for absolutely FREE and up to 1000 SMS alerts for $0.05 per SMS to a maximum of $50 per month. Your 1000 SMS alert limit is reset every month.

For example - If you signed up to Restock Rocket's PLUS plan on Dec 12, then your billing cycles will be Dec 12 - Jan 11, Jan 12 - Feb 11 and so on. Let's say you had 100 SMS notifications from Dec 12 - Jan 11, you will be charged $5 ($0.05 X 100) in the same billing cycle. You 1000 SMS limit will be reset on Jan 12th. If no SMS's are sent in a particular month, you will not be charged.
